Basic Cheesecake

Description

A basic cheesecake with creamy and rich top as well as a crunchy bottom. This recipe will take around 2 hours to bake but cooling time may be longer. Cheesecake can be cooled in either a few hours to an overnight period unless of course the temptation is just too strong. This recipe will produce around 16 servings in total.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Graham Cracker crumbs
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 5 tbsp melted butter
  • 8 oz of cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. To begin preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, brown sugar, cinnamon, and melted butter.
  3. Mix well and press mixture into the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.
  4. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes
  5. In a large bowl, beat together cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  6. Beat in sour cream and heavy cream.
  7. Blend in flour and vanilla.
  8. With a mixer on low speed, add eggs to mixture on at a time, then pour whole batter over crust.
  9. Bake in the oven for 60 to 70 minutes (or until center is almost set/filling is soft).
  10. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight before removing from pan.
  11. Slice and Serve.
