Flan

Description

A rich and creamy custard dessert with a caramel taste. Typically made with a caramel top and a custard like bottom. Made using dairy and animal products. Will serve up to 6-8 dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can of evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Melt sugar in a medium sauce pan over a medium low heat until golden.
  3. Pour the liquid into a 9 inch wide round baking dish. Turn dish around to evenly coat the sides. This must be done with caution as the pan will be very hot. Once done, set aside.
  4. Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Then mix in the condensed milk, evaporated milk and vanilla until smooth. Once done, pour the mixture into the previously coated baking dish and cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Place the dish into the oven and bake for approximately 60 minutes. Optional: Place the baking dish inside a waterbath (roasting pan with about 1 inch of water) before placing both in the oven.
  6. Once done, let the dish cool completly. This can be done by placing the dish into the fridge and waiting an extra 1-2 hours.
  7. Once cooled take a large enough brimmed plate and flip the dish carefully onto it. The flan should fall onto the plate smoothly. If not give the dish a few taps or slowly shake the dish side to side to release the edges.
  8. Slice and Serve.
